About Us

Where art
meets skin

"Every tattoo tells a story — we're here to make yours worth telling."

House of Calvary is a dedicated tattoo studio based in Singapore, built on a foundation of craft, intention, and trust. We believe a tattoo is more than ink — it's a permanent expression of who you are.

Our team of three artists — Sherman, Mica, and Nicholas — each bring a distinct style and depth of skill. From sweeping Japanese compositions to delicate florals and intricate blackwork, we cover a wide range of styles under one roof.

Every client walks in with an idea. We make sure they leave with something they'll carry proudly for life.
